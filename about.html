Mission and Vision

Our mission is to raise awareness of Alzheimer's disease and other types of dementia in the Philippines, working hand-in-hand with the official organization dedicated to the advocacy of the said disease, Alzheimer’s Disease Association of the Philippines (ADAP).

The target readers are mainly the people surrounding those who have Alzheimer's or other forms of dementia, relaying crucial information so that people can have a basic understanding of what happens and how their mind and struggles work, and that they know how to act around victims of the disease.

We want to provide affected people with a good experience so that they will not be stressed out given their condition, and in turn allow them to live a happy life like normal human beings.

Purple is our signature color, combining the calm stability of blue and the passionate energy of red. Purple makes a statement about our Association and our supporters: we are strong and unrelenting in the fight against Alzheimer’s disease.

Inspiration

The developer's inspiration for creating this website is his parents. His father was diagnosed with Alzheimer's disease while his mother was diagnosed with a form of hereditary dementia. Both of them have other conditions aside from these.

The developer only wishes that they are able live a happy life despite their condition, and that can only be achieved through the actions of the people around them.

Hopefully someday technological advancement in medicine and research can find a way to have something for the cure of the disease.
